Just Saw a Sign?


We and our partners are working to grow crops on public land for public harvesting.

We would love that you harvest a snack or even a meal from our plots. We just ask that you harvest properly and at the right time to maximize the utility of our efforts.

To learn how to harvest and whether the crop is ready, enter the site number or find the site on the map on the Locate a Site page.

About Us


We are a group of students striving for productive use of both land and waste by providing accessibility to compost bins and live crops to our community. As the gap between farms and consumers widens, we bring farming to the city to provide free produce anyone is willing to reap the rewards.

Let's disperse crops all over this city so we can graze over it. To learn more about how to grow with us, become a partner, or simply compost, click Get Involved.
