About Us

We are a group of students who fight for proper use of land and waste by providing compost bins and live crops to the community. We believe that there is a fresher, cheaper, and more sustainable alternative to buying overpriced grocery store food even in the city.
It may not look like there is space in the city for a garden because it has been traditionally unproductive but look around.
  • Landscaping on campus is covered with non-productive plants like grass and misc trees.
  • There is a lot of wasted space in landscaping filled with gravel and mulch.
  • Every building has concrete-covered balconies and patios.
Lets disperse crops all over this city so we can graze over it. To learn more about how to get involved, with the crew and start seeding, composting, and growing click Get Involved.
