Just Saw a Sign?

We and our partners are working to grow crops on public land for public harvesting.
We just ask that you harvest properly and at the right time to maximize the utility of our efforts.

To learn how to harvest and whether the crop is ready, enter the site number or find it on the map of the Locate a Site page.



New to Plant Care and Composting?

If you want to learn more about your farm, visit My Account and we will teach you everything you need to know about your plants.
If you want to learn about public plantings and compost bins, visit Locate a Site.



About Us

We are a group of students working striving for productive use of both land and waste by providing accessibility to compost bins and live crops in our community. As the gap between farms and consumers widens, we bring our farm to the city and provide free fresh produce to whoever is willing to harvest.

Let's disperse crops all over this city so we can graze over it. To learn more about how to get involved, with the crew and start seeding, composting, and growing click Get Involved.
