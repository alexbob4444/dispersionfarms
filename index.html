Just Saw a Sign?


We and our partners are working to grow beautiful and useful plants in public and private underutilized spaces for public use.

We would love that you harvest please from our plots; we just ask that you harvest properly and at the right time to maximize the utility of our efforts.

To learn how to harvest and whether a plant is ready, enter the site number or find the site on the map on the Locate a Site page.



New to Plant Care and Composting?


If you just recieved a farm and want to learn more about it, visit My Account and we will teach you everything you need to know. Just log in and select the plant you wish to know about.

If you would like a bucket farm on your south facing balcony or a garden on your property, log in or create an account and place a request.



About Us


We are a group of students striving for productive use of land, space, and waste by providing accessibility to composting and gardening in our community. As the gap between farms and consumers widens, we bring gardening to the city to inspire plant passion.

To learn more about how to grow with us or become a partner click Get Involved.
